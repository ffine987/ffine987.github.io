Why Faith/Arcane builds whip butt in Elden Ring

This is my page and now you have to read what it says, sorry sucker. Elden Ring is one of the coolest games ever and the coolest build of all is Faith/Arcane. It's by far the most versatile build in the game, and I've tried them all. On one hand, you get access to three elements plus a myriad of utility incantations from Faith. On the other hand, you get to use arcane-scaling weapons, which deal insane amounts of bleed damage, evaporating most enemies. Then on top of that, you get special access to DRAGON INCANTATIONS, that let you summon the awesome powers of ancient dragons to smite your foes. These spells are incredibly pricy in terms of your mana, but oh so worth it, even just for the grand spectacle of summoning a massive dragon head to roast your foes to a crisp. Check out this wiki page for my favorite spell in the game, that involves summoning a massive dragon arm from the sky to crush your enemies into dust.
